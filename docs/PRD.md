# ANOVIC Growth OS

## Product Requirements Document (PRD)

**Version:** 1.0

## Vision

ANOVIC Growth OS is a Business Intelligence platform that helps marketing agencies analyze businesses, generate digital audits, create growth strategies, and produce sales assets automatically.

## Objectives

- Analyze any business in less than 30 minutes.
- Generate Digital Audit.
- Generate Executive Report.
- Generate Sales Assets.
- Build a Knowledge Base.

## Modules

- Lead Intelligence
- Audit Engine
- Scoring Engine
- Competitor Intelligence
- Growth Intelligence
- Report Generator
- Sales Intelligence

## MVP

- Add Lead
- Audit Business
- Calculate Score
- Generate Report
- Generate WhatsApp
